KOREAN FAMOUS FOOD

"RED RICE CAKE-Tteokbokki"


VARIATIONS:


  • curry-tteokbokki

  • cream sauce-tteokbokki

  • jajang-tteokbokki

  • seafood-tteokbokki

  • rose-tteokbokki

  • galbi-tteokbokki

ABOUT:

Tteokbokki is a spicy stir-fried dish that usually consists of cylinder-shaped rice cakes, sweet red chili sauce, and fish cakes.
Originally, tteokbokki was called tteok jjim, a braised dish of sliced rice cakes, meat, eggs, and seasonings. Tteokbokki is often served with a broth.
Most people eat tteokbokki with some fried foods (튀김) or sundae (순대) which is a blood sausage (not really a blood sausage).
It is often accompanied with a broth that you sometimes have to ask for depending on the store.
